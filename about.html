Our Story

Fit-24/7 was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide a welcoming and inclusive space for people to pursue their fitness goals. Our founder, John Smith, was a lifelong fitness enthusiast who had experienced firsthand the frustration of gyms that were overcrowded, unwelcoming, and intimidating.

John saw an opportunity to create something different - a gym that was open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, with a wide variety of equipment and amenities to suit every fitness level and interest. But more than that, he wanted Fit-24/7 to be a place where everyone felt comfortable and supported, regardless of their age, gender, or fitness background.

Over the years, Fit-24/7 has grown into a thriving community of fitness enthusiasts who share John's vision. Our team of experienced trainers and staff are dedicated to helping every member achieve their goals, whether that's improving their health, building muscle, losing weight, or simply feeling more confident and energized.

We believe that fitness is about more than just working out - it's about building relationships, pushing yourself to new heights, and becoming the best version of yourself. At Fit-24/7, we're proud to be a part of that journey for each and every one of our members.

Our Goals

  • To help every member achieve their fitness goals, no matter their starting point
  • To provide a welcoming and inclusive community for all fitness levels and backgrounds
  • To offer a wide variety of equipment and amenities to suit every interest and need
  • To promote healthy habits and lifestyle changes that last beyond the gym
  • To continually improve our services and facilities to meet the evolving needs of our members

Our second goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive community for all fitness levels and backgrounds. We want everyone who walks through our doors to feel welcome and supported. We strive to create a positive, inclusive environment where our members can feel comfortable, confident, and motivated to achieve their goals.

Thirdly, we aim to provide a wide variety of equipment and amenities to suit every interest and need. From cardio machines and free weights to group fitness classes and personal training sessions, we have everything our members need to achieve their fitness goals.

Fourthly, we are committed to promoting healthy habits and lifestyle changes that last beyond the gym. We believe that fitness is not just about what you do in the gym, but also about making healthy choices in your daily life. That's why we offer resources and support to help our members make positive changes to their diet, sleep habits, and overall lifestyle.

Finally, we are dedicated to continually improving our services and facilities to meet the evolving needs of our members. We listen to feedback from our members and strive to provide the best possible experience for everyone who walks through our doors.

These goals are at the heart of everything we do at Fit-24/7, and we're committed to helping our members achieve their fitness goals and lead healthier, happier lives.
